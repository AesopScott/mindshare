Phase 00 is where the process becomes real.

Before the lifecycle can guide work, the team needs agreement on what the lifecycle is, what each phase produces, and where the artifacts live.

01

Name the lifecycle

MAPS means Multi-Agent Pipeline Skills. The name anchors the operating frame and the repo.

02

Agree on phases

The sequence starts with alignment, then moves through define, design, build, equip, evaluate, deploy, observe, and improve.

03

Scaffold artifacts

Each phase gets a guide, a skill, and at least one template so planning and agent execution stay connected.

04

Make room to grow

The first page establishes the structure. Later pages can go deep on one phase at a time without crowding the map.
