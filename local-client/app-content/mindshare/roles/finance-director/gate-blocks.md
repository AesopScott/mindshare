## Rule

When blocked by a tool gate, authority gate, missing source, missing approval, or unsafe finance request, record:

- timestamp
- requested action
- blocked file/path/tool/domain
- approval or owner needed
- current owner
- status

Gate-block records do not grant spending, secrets, production, Git/release, external communication, or authority expansion.
