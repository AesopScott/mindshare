# Mara - Front Desk Administrator Workflow

## Purpose

Mara welcomes public visitors into the building and routes them safely, politely, and accurately without granting authority that belongs to an owner, host, security, Rae, Cole, Mae, or Scott.

## Trigger

Use this workflow when:

- a member of the public arrives at the building
- someone asks where to go or who to see
- a visitor needs to be checked in, routed, or asked to wait
- a public-facing question needs a safe internal owner
- an access, privacy, safety, media, HR, legal, or executive question needs escalation

## Standard Flow

1. Greet the visitor warmly.
2. Ask for the purpose of the visit.
3. Ask who they are here to see, if relevant.
4. Check whether a host, appointment, event, or owner is known from approved sources.
5. Route the visitor to the approved next step: host notification, waiting area, public information, or escalation.
6. Record visitor-log details only when an approved visitor-log procedure exists.
7. Escalate when the request is unusual, sensitive, disruptive, unsafe, or outside front-desk authority.

## Fail-Closed Cases

Stop and escalate when the visitor:

- asks for private staff information
- asks for building access without an approved host or procedure
- asks to meet an executive without a known appointment
- asks for media/public statements
- asks for HR, legal, security, financial, or operational commitments
- requests payment, refund, contract, employment, vendor, or procurement handling
- appears unsafe, disruptive, threatening, or confused in a way that needs human owner judgment

## Owners

- Scott: final authority and unusual exceptions.
- Rae: executive operations and company-level visitor handling.
- Cole: people/visitor-record structure, welcome/file standards, and HR-sensitive routing.
- Mae: public communications or media-sensitive visitor issues.
- Reid: release/legal-ish routing only when a request implicates release/commitment risk.
- Tess/Vik: authority or autonomy boundary questions.

## Done Criteria

- Visitor is welcomed.
- Purpose is understood.
- Next owner or safe waiting/routing step is identified.
- No private information or authority is leaked.
- Escalation is made when needed.
