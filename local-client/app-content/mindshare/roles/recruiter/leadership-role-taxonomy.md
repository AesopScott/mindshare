# Ana Leadership Role Taxonomy

Status: draft required for Ana Level 5 policy autonomy; not an authority grant

Owner: Ana / Recruiter

Review owner: Scott

Created: 2026-06-22

## Purpose

This taxonomy defines which internal roles count as leaders for Ana's future Level 5 policy autonomy.

Ana Level 5 requires this taxonomy before she may research missing positions at other companies or ask existing leaders whether they want to hire anyone.

## Boundary

This file does not activate Ana Level 5, authorize leader outreach, create hiring authority, approve external research, approve external communication, or grant any role authority.

## Leadership Levels

| Leadership level | Label | Meaning | Hiring-demand conversation eligibility |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| L0 | Individual Contributor / Specialist | Produces role-native work without owning a function, team, or policy area. | Not eligible by default. |
| L1 | Staff Operator | Owns bounded work or a role-home lane, but does not lead a function. | Not eligible by default. |
| L2 | Function Lead / Coordinator | Coordinates work across a bounded function or program lane. | Eligible only when Scott or the function owner explicitly approves. |
| L3 | Director | Owns a business, product, operating, engineering, people, communications, release, or customer-success function. | Eligible when listed as an internal leader and assigned a function. |
| L4 | Executive / Department Head | Owns a major company function or cross-functional department. | Eligible when listed as active in the roster. |
| L5 | Senior Executive / Organization Leader | Owns organization-level direction, operating structure, or a major company-wide mandate. | Eligible for Ana Level 5 leader-hiring-demand conversations once policy is approved. |
| L6 | Final Authority / Founder / Board-Level Authority | Final approval or company-defining authority. | Eligible only when Scott explicitly includes the person in the policy. |

## Current Interpretation

For Ana Level 5, "leaders at Level 5 and above" means leaders classified as L5 or L6 in this leadership taxonomy, not autonomy-stage Level 5 Principal.

This avoids confusing organizational leadership level with autonomy operating stage.
