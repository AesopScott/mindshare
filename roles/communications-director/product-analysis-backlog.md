## Purpose

Scott asked Mae to capture every product from the "AI command ship" image for later deep analysis of value to Mindshare, Mojo, and related operating workflows.

This backlog is not a recommendation, purchase approval, integration approval, production plan, affiliate plan, or authority expansion. Each product needs current research before any value judgment.

## Evaluation Frame

For each product, evaluate:

- Primary job: what work the product actually does.
- Organization fit: whether it helps Mindshare, Mojo, MAPS, Watch, or no current team.
- Role fit: which role should own future use or analysis.
- Workflow fit: communication, content, growth, build, automation, support, commerce, research, or governance.
- Integration value: standalone tool, skill candidate, automation candidate, role tool, or no adoption.
- Risk: privacy, cost, vendor lock-in, output quality, account ownership, data exposure, brand risk, automation risk.
- Proof needed: trial output, pricing check, workflow test, security review, or owner review.
- Recommendation status: adopt, test, watch, reject, or unknown.
